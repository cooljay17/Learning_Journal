## Key Takeaways

✨ Attended another confidence-building session as part of **Infosys Springboard – Pragati: Path to Future | Cohort 9** on **"Building & Unleashing Your Self-Confidence"** by **Surekha Rayappan**.

The session was insightful and packed with practical ideas to help build self-confidence in everyday life. 🌟

The concept that resonated with me the most was the **"Brag Book"** 📖✨—a personal journal where we record our achievements, big or small.

Why it stood out:

- 🌱 Helps acknowledge and celebrate personal progress.
- 💪 Acts as a confidence booster during moments of self-doubt.
- 🧠 Reminds us of our strengths when we're caught in a negative thought loop.
- 🚀 Encourages us to keep moving forward by reflecting on how far we've come.

Sometimes, we focus so much on what we haven't achieved that we forget everything we already have. Maintaining a **Brag Book** is a simple yet powerful way to recognize our growth and build lasting self-confidence.

> Celebrate your wins, trust your journey, and let your progress remind you of your potential. ✨
