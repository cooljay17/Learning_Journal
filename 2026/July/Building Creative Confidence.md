## Key Takeaways

Today's session was a gentle reminder that **creative confidence isn't about knowing everything—it's about believing your perspective has value.**

I especially connected with the idea that confidence grows through:

- ✔️ Preparation
- ✔️ Practice
- ✔️ Persistence

The discussion on overcoming the fear of being judged, taking the first step, and embracing the unknown made me reflect on how easily silence can become a habit. And when it does, it slowly gives space to self-doubt.

I'm choosing to change that.

> Here's to becoming a little more curious, a little more vocal, and a little more like my **"Creative Confident Me"** every day. 🌱
